Genres

Action
Films that are fast paced, often starring a central hero or heroine and including scenes of epic battles, disasters, or rescues.
Comedy
Movies with light-hearted plots designed to make the audience laugh, through jokes, characters, and actions.
Science Fiction
Movies that include scientific-esque elements, often including space travel, aliens, or futuristic settings.
